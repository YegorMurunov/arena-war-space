Fight & Earn

Arena is a PvP mode of battle between players online. Take part in a deadly battle against a powerful enemy of the Warspace universe. Fights are scheduled regularly and at specific times, you can find links to them on this page or on our social networks.
    How to fight:
  • You have 30 seconds to decide whether to strike the boss or to wait for a more opportune moment
  • Each attack costs 1 WAX
  • After each attack time resets back to 30 sec
  • The one who does the last hit and stays 30 sec without anybody's else hit - gets the reward!
*To join the fight you need to have some waxes, please register WAX-wallet if you don't have it.
